Our Achievements

Since competitions resumed after the COVID-19 pandemic, Mid Essex Boxing Club has made extraordinary strides, punching well above its weight for a club of our size. Our dedicated boxers have showcased their skills at national competitions and prestigious Box Cups across the country. Below, we proudly highlight some of our most significant achievements;

England Boxing National Championships

  • 1 x National Champion (Elin-Rose Chapman)
  • 2 x National Finalists (Elin-Rose Chapman, Manny Mohammed)
  • 4 x National Semi-finalists (Elin-Rose Chapman, Manny Mohammed, Tommy Pemberton, Megan King)
  • 5 x National Quarter-finalists (2 x Manny Mohammed, 3 x Tyler O'Connor)

NABGC National Championships

  • 1 x National Champion (Megan King)
  • 1 x National Finalist (Sophia Sanchez)
  • 2 x Quarter-finalists (2 x Junior Gullam)

Eastern Counties Championships

  • 1 x Eastern Counties Champion (TJ Jarvis-Brown)
  • 3 x Eastern Counties Finalists (Tamuno Bamson, Luca Sechi)

Box Cups

  • 1 x Box Cup Champion (Megan King)
  • 2 x Box Cup Finalists (Sophia Sanchez, Luca Sechi)
  • 1 x Box Cup Semi-finalist (Sophia Sanchez)
